Christ Rules Everything Around Me (C.R.E.A.M.)

A business that was created to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ through inspirational apparel. We need to have complete certainty that things will work out, not because we are righteous or wise, but because of the time, the effort, the prayers, and the tools we are using. From the moment we are given awareness about some bigger picture or mission, we have to have complete focus on what to do to get to that place. -Yehuda Berg On May 18, 2018 God placed C.R.E.R.M. Apparel on my heart. I didn’t know what I was getting myself into. I thought to myself Lord, I can’t do this, you definitely got the wrong person for this mission. I never wanted to be a entrepreneur of any business. This all came about when I accidentally misread someone’s T-shirt. That day God replaced cash with Christ. As I leaned not on my own understand, rather trusting God and the mission he placed before me C. R.E.A.M. Apparel skyrocketed. I sold more then a thousand dollars worth of t-shirts, through this profit I was able to allocate a percentage of what was sold to those in need under a year’s time. One thing that I have learned from this encounter with God, is that he will always have the final say. C.R.E.A.M. Apparel become a walking inspiration.
